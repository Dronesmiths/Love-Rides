About the LOVE RIDEÂ®

Love RideÂ® began in 1984 as a grassroots motorcycle event driven by a simple mission: to support those in need. Over three decades, it evolved into the largest and most respected single day motorcycle charity event in the world, to date raising more than $25 million for a wide range of causes.

Love Ride is organized by motorcycle riders for motorcycle riders as a celebration of freedom, goodwill, music, and community. Over the years celebrity supporters and riders such as Jay Leno, Steven Tyler, Peter Fonda, Brian Cranston, Robert Patrick, Dennis Hopper, Lorenzo Lamas, Willie G. Davidson and many others have helped elevate its reach, joined by 10s of thousands of motorcycle enthusiasts from across Southern California and beyond coming together to make a positive impact creating a cultural event icon. Each edition of the Love Ride has included live music with past performers including Foo Fighters, ZZ Top, Billy Idol, Jackson Browne, Social Distortion, Robby Krieger and many more coupled with motorcycle stunt exhibitions, bike shows, food and good times benefiting great causes.
